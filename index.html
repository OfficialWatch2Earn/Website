What is Watch2Earn?

Watch2Earn (W2E) is a meme coin that disrupts traditional advertising by allowing the community to share in revenue generated by content creators. Whether from YouTube, mobile apps, or websites, a portion of ad earnings flows back into W2E. This unique approach fuels sustainable growth, encouraging both viewers and creators to be active participants in the ecosystem.
